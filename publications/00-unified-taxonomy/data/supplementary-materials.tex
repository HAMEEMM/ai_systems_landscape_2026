\subsection{Inclusion and Exclusion Criteria}

\textbf{Inclusion criteria:}
\begin{itemize}
  \item Peer-reviewed journal articles, conference papers, or workshop proceedings
  \item Published between January 2015 and December 2025
  \item Written in English
  \item Indexed in at least one of the four target databases
\end{itemize}

\textbf{Exclusion criteria:}
\begin{itemize}
  \item Non-peer-reviewed preprints, blog posts, or technical reports
  \item Duplicate records (removed via DOI matching and title similarity with Levenshtein distance $\leq$ 0.05)
  \item Publications with fewer than 4 pages (to exclude abstracts and poster descriptions)
  \item Publications not primarily focused on AI system design or methodology
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Deduplication Process}
Records were first matched on DOI (exact match). Remaining records without DOIs were deduplicated using normalised title comparison (lowercase, punctuation-removed) with a Levenshtein distance threshold of 0.05. This yielded a final corpus of 15,427 unique publications from an initial pool of 21,893 raw records (29.5\% duplication rate across databases).

\subsection{Top Qualitative Feedback Themes}

\begin{enumerate}
  \item \textbf{Agentic vs. Autonomous boundary} (mentioned by 14/47 respondents): Respondents requested sharper criteria for distinguishing goal-directed agency from reactive autonomy.
  \item \textbf{XAI as overlay vs. standalone} (mentioned by 11/47): Several respondents noted XAI could be viewed as a meta-capability that applies across types rather than a standalone category.
  \item \textbf{Missing category: World-model-based AI} (mentioned by 9/47): Respondents suggested adding systems centred on internal world models (e.g., Sora, JEPA).
  \item \textbf{Temporal evolution} (mentioned by 7/47): Interest in how system types evolve and converge over time.
  \item \textbf{Positive reception of three-dimensional model} (mentioned by 31/47): The architecture--capability--deployment framework was highlighted as a particular strength.
\end{enumerate}
